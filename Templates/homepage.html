Improving Reading Comprehension: Assistive, Virtual, and Educative web based Software (AVES)

Project Overview

The main objective of this study is to design, develop, implement, and evaluate a system with a multi-modal approach, incorporating quiet reading, loud reading, question generation, and comprehension assessment functionalities utilizing natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning (ML) to enhance the reading comprehension of students.
